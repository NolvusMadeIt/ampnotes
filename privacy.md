# AMP Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AMP (All My Prompts) handles data.

## 1. Core Privacy Model

AMP is designed as a desktop-first app. Prompt content, settings, and installed marketplace assets are primarily stored locally on your device.

## 2. Data Types AMP May Store Locally

- profile display preferences
- prompt content and metadata
- tags, folders, and templates
- theme/plugin manifests and installed asset state
- update preferences and UI state
- verified purchase markers (when license verification is used)

## 3. External Network Calls

AMP may call external services only when features require them, such as:

- AI provider endpoints (for refine/validation integrations)
- payment/license verification endpoints (for paid marketplace flows)
- release/update metadata endpoints
- user-configured marketplace URLs

## 4. Credentials and Keys

When API keys or secure values are configured, AMP uses local secure storage mechanisms where available.

You are responsible for securing your local device and accounts used with third-party integrations.

## 5. Third-Party Services

If you use integrations (for example Groq, Gumroad, GitHub, Vercel), those providers process data under their own privacy policies.

AMP maintainers do not control third-party data practices.

## 6. Data Sharing

AMP does not sell your personal data.

Data is shared externally only through:

- your explicit export/share actions
- configured integrations
- update checks and release lookups

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete prompts, templates, themes, plugins, and profile settings from within the app or by removing app data locally.

## 8. Security

AMP includes schema validation and import guards for manifests and marketplace payloads, but no software can guarantee absolute security.

Use trusted sources for imported assets and keep your app updated.

## 9. Children's Privacy

AMP is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the app in violation of local age or consent requirements.

## 10. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of revised policy terms.

## 11. Contact

For privacy questions, use the repository support channels.
