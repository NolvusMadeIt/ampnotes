# AMP Refund Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

AMP desktop software is currently provided free of charge.

## Software Refunds

Because AMP is free software, there is no software purchase fee to refund.

## Marketplace / Third-Party Purchases

If you purchase third-party assets (for example marketplace themes/plugins sold through external payment providers), refund terms are determined by:

- the seller's policy
- the payment provider's policy

Please review the listing terms before purchase.

## Contact

For software support issues, use the project support/repository channels.
